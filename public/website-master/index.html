Luxury homepage motive

Full scenic hero, refined slogan, and a controlled booking CTA. This is the premium visual language for fine dining and destination restaurants.

Basic homepage motive

Homepage should convert quickly with visible prices, big dish images, and direct call-to-order behavior. Signature cards below are the main selling surface.

Minimal homepage motive

Use editorial whitespace, a calm story-led opening, and fewer but stronger dish highlights. The page should feel designed, not crowded.

Diner homepage motive

Use louder urgency, stronger contrast, and direct order intent. Categories and the floating cart become part of the homepage conversion flow.

Homepage Signatures

Signature dishes for basic and modern homepage feeds

On basic themes this is the front-page sales wall. On minimal themes it becomes a curated selection with more breathing room. Luxury can keep it shorter. Diner themes should lean more on categories below.
