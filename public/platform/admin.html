This is the platform-side admin surface. It is intentionally simpler than tenant admin: website builder defaults, pricing, signup CRM-lite, and follow-up workflow.

Access

Use the operator admin PIN. Default preview PIN on seeded data is 1234.

Platform Pricing

Global SaaS-side pricing, tier copy, and public feature bullets used on the marketing website and signup flow.

Online

Service

POS and service-flow tier.

Repeat Guests

CRM and guest retention tier.

Groups

Enterprise-style multi-site tier. Leave price empty to keep it custom-contact only.

Payment Method Controls

These toggles control which payment methods may appear in self-serve signup and tenant payment setup.

Signup CRM Lite

Recent self-serve signups. Follow up manually for now; automation can be layered later.

Moderation Review Queue

Pending and recent website moderation decisions. Use the operator PIN loaded above to approve, reject, suspend, or quarantine directly.

Inbound Contact Leads
