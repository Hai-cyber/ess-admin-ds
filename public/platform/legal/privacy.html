2. What data we collect

3. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — processing necessary to provide the service you subscribed to.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — security monitoring, fraud prevention, service improvement.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR) — compliance with applicable laws (e.g., tax records, TSE/fiscal obligations).
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — where explicitly requested (e.g., marketing emails).

4. How we use your data

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Restaurant OS platform.
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, invoices, service notifications).
  • Send SMS notifications to your guests on your behalf (via Twilio, acting as sub-processor).
  • Fraud prevention and security monitoring.
  • Compliance with legal obligations.

We do not sell your data or your guests' data to any third party.

5. Data processors & sub-processors

  • Cloudflare, Inc. — infrastructure (Workers, D1, CDN). Data hosted in Germany / EU where possible.
  • Stripe, Inc. — payment processing. Subject to Stripe's own privacy policy.
  • Twilio, Inc. — SMS delivery for guest notifications.
  • SendGrid (Twilio) — transactional email delivery.
  • Fiskaly GmbH — TSE (fiscal signing) for German-law compliance.

6. Data retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active, plus 30 days after cancellation.
  • Booking/guest data: retained per your active subscription, then deleted 30 days after account closure.
  • Financial/invoice records: retained for 10 years (German tax law requirement).
  • Log data: max 90 days, then automatically purged.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your data where no legal obligation requires retention.
  • Restriction — request we limit how we process your data.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@restaurantos.app. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In Germany: Bundesbeauftragte für den Datenschutz (BfDI).

8. Cookies

The Platform uses only technically necessary cookies (session authentication). No third-party tracking cookies are used. No consent banner is required for strictly necessary cookies.

9. Data transfers outside the EU

Some sub-processors (Stripe, Twilio, Cloudflare) are headquartered in the United States. Data transfers are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as approved by the European Commission.

10. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures: HTTPS-only traffic, encrypted data at rest, tenant isolation in the database, PIN-based authentication, audit logging of all data mutations. No system is 100% secure; please report any suspected security issues to security@restaurantos.app.

11. Changes to this policy
